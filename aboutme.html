About Me

This is my personal space to share my passions, projects, and the journey I’ve been on. Whether you’re here to explore my work or just browsing, I hope you find something that resonates with you.

Ever since I was a kid, I’ve been captivated by computers and technology but it all really started with video games. Playing games wasn’t just fun for me; it sparked a curiosity about how they worked behind the scenes. I remember wondering how worlds were built, characters were animated, and mechanics were programmed.

That early passion for gaming eventually led me down the path of programming. I wanted to create, not just consume. Over time, I began learning how to build my own games and applications, turning that curiosity into real skills. Today, I’m pursuing a degree in Computer Science at UMBC, and I’ve had the opportunity to work at the Apple Store, where I’ve gained insight into both the technical and business sides of the tech world.

I’m driven by that same sense of wonder that video games sparked in me years ago and I’m excited to see where it will lead.
